Our Resources

The Computer Science Department has a lot of resources from libraries to software labs and other important resources that help students to make researchs and learn more.

  • Department Library
  • Software Lab
  • Lecture's Office
  • Conference Room
Software lab

This software lab is a place where students from the computer science department can meet and work on their assignments and projects. The software lab has sockets and elecricity so students can work on their laptop for longer hours while on campus.Such an environment also promotes collabration and team work as people can ask their mates to help them with any sort of challenge they are facing in the course of doing their projects.
Location: Ground floor,adjacent the car park

Conference Roooms

The conference rooms are used during presentation of projects at the department by both students and lecturers. This conference room is very important as it makes presentation very exciting. It has all the devices you can think of in presentation.
Location:First floor,Opposite the PHD library

Library

The department library is a place where students read and do indepth analysis into what they learn in class. They also get the chance to access books to read and broaden their understanding in topics that are complex and difficult to understand
location: Near the Software Lab

Lectures' Office
The department has offices for lecturers.This amkes it very easy for students to find their lecturers when facing a particular academic problem and need support from the lecturers. The ease of finding and talking to lecturers of the department exposes students to a lot of information and opportunities.
Location:Main block , first floor

All these resources the department has go a long way to improve the academic performance of the studentsand also their skills in whatever field of computer science. This makes them get an upper hand in the world of work to face the competitive job market after graduating.Some students are able to get their jobs even before graduating.
